# Roadmap

Aura OS product roadmap by phase. Phases are sequential; later phases build on earlier foundations.

## Phase 1

Core operating system for planners — authenticate, run the business day-to-day, and manage the wedding lifecycle.

- **Authentication** — Sign up, sign in, session management, and protected routes
- **Dashboard** — Command Center overview (meetings, weddings, tasks, finance signals)
- **Clients** — CRM for client records, status, and follow-ups
- **Weddings** — Wedding projects linked to clients, dates, venues, and status
- **Tasks** — Prioritized work items tied to weddings and clients
- **Meetings** — Scheduled meetings with clients / weddings
- **Finance** — Revenue, expenses, and payments

## Phase 2

Deeper wedding operations — planning surfaces, vendors, client-facing delivery, and media.

- **Timeline** — Day-of and planning timelines per wedding
- **Vendor Management** — Vendor directory, assignments, and coordination
- **Wedding Portal** — Shared wedding workspace for planners and stakeholders
- **File Upload** — Document and asset uploads via Supabase Storage
- **Gallery** — Photo / media galleries per wedding
- **Calendar** — Unified calendar across meetings, tasks, and wedding dates

## Phase 3

Intelligence, reach, and growth — AI, client self-serve, alerts, mobile, and insight.

- **AI Assistant** — In-product assistant for planning and operations
- **Client Viewer Portal** — Read-focused portal for clients to view progress
- **Notifications** — In-app and push / email notifications
- **Mobile App** — Native or mobile-first companion experience
- **Analytics** — Business and wedding performance analytics

## Notes

- Phase 1 schema and Command Center landed in Sprint 001.
- Sprint 002 establishes documentation and architecture foundations before expanding Phase 1 feature depth.
